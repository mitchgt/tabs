Colin Hay Acoustic version of Overkill 
 
Though more work has gone in the writing of other verions  
of this tab I belive if you take the time to play it with both 
the CD and the preformance on the TV show SCRUBS 
you will find this to tab to be the closest yet. 
 
On SCRUBS Colin plays a blues jam before breaking into  
the song that shows he is using this tuning. 
 
This is an alternate tuning, First and Sixth stings tuned to D. 
 
This is a great blues slide tune, so try a slider on it after you  
learn the song and have some fun.
