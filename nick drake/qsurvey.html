The list below, taken from the February issue of Q, tells us at least two things about the Q readers: 1. They prefer British music 2. They have short memory (in some cases I'd say very short!), i.e. 54 records are from the 90's, only 19 from the 70's. The first conclusion helps to explain Nick's appearance, while the second one makes his entrance even more remarkable.
