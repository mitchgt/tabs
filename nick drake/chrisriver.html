notes:
1. the 2's on the G-string in the first bar (starred above)
might well be 4's, but the notes are quiet and I really can't make
up my mind! The discord you get with 4's is certainly part of the strings 
score, so you might like to use 4's more towards the end of the
song. See what you think.
2. the 33033x is a kind of Eb9 with the bass only as a passing note
- the double bass plays the (quiet) Eb in the track, which is not 
easy to duplicate unaccompanied! (thanks to Howard Wright for the
suggested fingering)
