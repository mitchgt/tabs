NICK DRAKE TRIBUTE NIGHT


There's going to be a Nick Drake Tribute Night on 25th November 1999 at The Marsden Theatre, Leyland, Preston, Lancs. All the proceeds are going to NW Mind (The Mental Health Charity). It starts around 8.00pm and will consist of two halves. The first half consists of primarily solo/duo acts performing ND numbers, and the second half will consist of a smaller numberof performers playing ND covers, but with NEW string arrangements written especially for the event, and played by a string quartet. Unlike some other events only Nick Drake songs are being played.



Performers involved include:

Michael Chapman - folk/rock legend who knew Nick Drake, with a catalogue of dozens of records.
Bill Pritchard - Francophile singer-songwriter and lover of chansons. Made an acclaimed series of albums in the eighties/early nineties.
Patsy Matheson - Leeds-based singer-songwriter signed to Tomorrow records.
Dave Gardner - Preston-based performer signed to Ammonite Records.
Christian Laverick - Recently signed up singer-songwriter from Lincoln.

Several semi-professional musicians with a strong affiliation to Nick Drake are also playing.
