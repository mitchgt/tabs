DRAKE: Way To Blue



WAY TO BLUE


I know it seems crazy to offer a guitar version of a string
piece, but I fancied having a go. Of course, these are
just approximations, but they're reasonably close and it's
fun to play so what the hell. The shapes are given lowest
string first, and are intended to be plucked together.
(x=don't play that string, and eg xx25(5/4)5 goes from
xx2555 to xx2545 during the bar). Tuning is standard.
