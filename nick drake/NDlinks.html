    MUSIC: SITE-RELATED LINKS

  • Scott Appel
    Songwriter and Nick Drake interpretor.
  • Arco
    Tabmaster Chris Healey's band Arco have released an excellent album called Coming To Terms.
  • Robin Frederick
    The author of several praised Nick Drake essays is also a high-class singer & songwriter.
  • Tom Hanson
    Drake-inspired singer and guitarist Tom Hanson's page offers MP3's from his Wake Of The Moon album. It's definitely worth checking out and if you like what you hear you can contact Tom at thanson@ecmadison.com for more information.
  • Gilbert Isbin
    Belgian guitar virtuoso who released an acclaimed cover album in 2000.
  • Slowjane
    Chicago-based band who took their name from a Nick Drake song.
  • Paul Wheeler
    Nick's childhood friend who wrote I Can Feel It, recorded by John Martyn.
