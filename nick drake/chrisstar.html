HANGING ON A STAR (corrected and updated 01/19/98)

Tuning BF#BEBD# (like CGCFCE or DADGDF# but lower)
no capo


This song involves some very simple chord shapes, but the picking
varies in detail all over the place. So as usual, here's one
version of the major ideas, but you should take it perhaps as a
guide rather than as gospel.
