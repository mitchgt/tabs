All of these natural harmonics are tricky. The 2<14> means fret the
2nd fret and pluck a natural harmonic on the 14th fret (which I guess
would make it an "artificial harmonic", but who cares about
semantics?). The best way to accomplish this is to fret all of the
notes you're going to need (in this case, the 1st fret on the B string
and the 2nd fret on the G) and then grab the pick with your thumb and
middle finger, which leaves your index finger to do the harmonic-
making. So, to get the <12>, put your right index finger over the 12th
fret and pluck behind it with your pick (being held by your middle
finger and thumb, remember?). Then use your ring finger to pluck the B
string, then the harmonic trick on the D string, ring finger on the
high E, and harmonic trick on the G string. Simple, eh? Okay, back to
the solo...
