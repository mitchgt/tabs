Other tunings are possible - DADGAD, open D or double drop D (all tuned one step lower, to C). The only chord that seems to require a 1st string in standard tuning, is F#m7. Throughout the rest of the song, the 1st string doesn't seem to be used.

The tabs below follow the re-tuned guitar, here and there incorporating the bass figure, which is not always played on that guitar. At the bottom of the page is a version in standard tuning, for those who don't want to re-tune your guitars.
