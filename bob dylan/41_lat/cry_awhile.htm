Cry Awhile

Words and music Bob Dylan
Released on Love And Theft (2001)
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

Capo 3rd fret (sounding key G major). It is of course possible to play it without a capo, but it sounds best with an E7 chord shape, like 022430 (half barre 2nd fret).

During the first part of the verses (the bouncy part), the guitars play a descending bass line (e-d-c#-b) against the ascending e-f#-g-g# in the bass.
