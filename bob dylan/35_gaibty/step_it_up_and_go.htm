Released on Good As I Been To You (1992)
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

Guitar tuned down one whole tone (sounding key thus F major)

The tab of the intro is at the same time the basic outline of the strumming for the verses. It is less complicated than it looks: the boogie woogie bass for the G, the little lick in bar three for the C (which comes a little late in the intro), and the turn to D at the end, which is not played in the intro, but in the verses.
The tab only notes the melodic bass notes that depart from the fundamental chord; the upstrokes generally strum the higher strings.
