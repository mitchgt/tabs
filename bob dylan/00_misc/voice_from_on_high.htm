Dylan's version

Dylan is closer to Bill Monroe's version than to the Stanley Brothers', both in the lyrics and in the turn to A7 (D7 in Monroe's version) in the verse.
If anyone can make out what he is (or should be) singing in the first verse, I'd be happy to receive a note. The only radical difference from the older versions is the end of the lines in the chorus: they hold them and weave a little something between the voices - Dylan & Co cut it short.
