Little Moses

Trad/A. P. Carter
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

Lyrics transcribed by Manfred Helfert, who also says:

Covered by Dylan during 5 Mar 1970 "Self Portrait" sessions (credited to Bert A. Williams/Earle C. Jones), during 1975 Rolling Thunder Revue tour (a fragment with Dylan on piano, probably dating from Nov 1975 is part of the "Renaldo and Clara" soundtrack), performed live in 1992 and 1993. Dylan's most likely source is The Carter Family's recording (RCA Studios, Camden, NJ, 14 Feb 1929, included in Harry Smith's Anthology of American Folk Music (track 53) on Folkways. (Roots, Routes and Ramblings)
