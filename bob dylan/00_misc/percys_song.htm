The turn G-G6-G7 is made up of the chords

G   320003
G6  322003
G7  323003

Dylan plays it with a hammer-on on both of the chord changes. In order to be played as smoothely as necessary and keep the high g on the first string, the G6 chord should be played with a mini-barré with the index finger on the 4th and 5th strings. An easier solution (and probably what Dylan does), is to leave the 5th string out and play 3x0003 - 3x2003 - 3x3003.
