Words and music Bob Dylan
Released on Blonde on Blonde (1966)
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

The recurring riff here is much more of a harp riff than a guitar riff, but it is playable on a guitar too, of course.
The harp lick (cross harp, harp key D major) is noted under the guitar tab (all notes are draw unless noted by a +. ' and " = bend one and two half steps down, respectively).

The first guitar figure in the intro is used for the first measures of the subsequent verses.
