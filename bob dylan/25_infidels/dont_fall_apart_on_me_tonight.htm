The G/b can be played x20003, or just as well (or better) x20010, and the Fs could be Fmaj7.
The long Gs at the end of the verses and (especially) the refrain are so filled with notes that giving an exact representation would be both impossible and futile. The progression C/g - G - F/g is close enough.
