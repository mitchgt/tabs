Down in the Flood (Crash on the Levee)

Words and music Bob Dylan
Released on The Basement Tapes (1967/1975). A different version was recorded Oct 1971 with Happy Traum (banjo) and released on Greatest Hits II (1971)
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

Take #1 (I assume): Capo 7th fret (sounding key D major); "another good friend" instead of "another best friend"
Take #2: Capo 2nd (or 3rd) fret (sounding key A (or Bb) major)
