Outtake version

Recorded June 2, 1970 (New Morning sessions)

One of the most beautiful tracks Dylan has ever recorded, and certainly the best version of this song, ever, with superbly loose piano playing.
The rhythm is at times a bit... eccentric - don't expect the rigours of the barlines to coincide with the phrasing.
It is played in Ab major, so the best option on a guitar is to put a capo on the first fret and play in G.
The chord that I have called Bm seems at times to be played Bm7+5 (x2403x), at times even as a G/b.

D11 = xx0010
