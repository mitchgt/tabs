Shooting Star

Words and music Bob Dylan.
Released on Oh Mercy (1989) and in a live version on Unplugged (1995).
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

B'' (in the bridge) is a chord with a whole lot of tones in it, on several guitars, playing quite different chords. A good approximation is x22324.

In the outtake version the song is clearly played in C, with a capo on the 4th fret (see below). On Unplugged Dylan plays his licks without a capo, while Jackson uses the capo on the 4th fret.
