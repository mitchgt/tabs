Knockin' on Heaven's Door

Words and music Bob Dylan
Released on Pat Garret & Billy The Kid (1973) and on Biograph (1985), and in live versions on Before the Flood (1974), At Budokan (1978), Dylan and the Dead (1988), MTV Unplugged (1995), and Live 1975 (2002)
Tabbed and transcribed by Eyolf Østrem

The challenge with this song is not the chords, which are quite simple, actually, but the lyrics, which change quite a lot, almost from show to show, with improvised new lines about what brings him to the door, knockin'.

The Am7 is played as a simple Am in most versions.
