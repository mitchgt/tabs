Move Away Jimmy Blue 

Hi. Iain showed me how to play this after a gig in '98,
So it should be pretty accurate.

As always, I welcome any comments or corrections,


You will need to re-tune your guitar from standard EADGBE to GBDGBD(Dobro)as follows:

(low)E>  G
     A>  B
     D>  D
     G>  G 
     B>  B
     E>  D 

I'm not sure of the namings, but the chords used are as follows
