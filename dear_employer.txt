The Minus 5-"Dear Employer (The Reason I Quit)"
from the album "Down with Wilco"

Capo 2nd fret, all chords relative to the capo.

For most of the song (all the "dear employer" verses), the chord progression is:

D A Em G

The timing of those chords goes something like this:
Dear em-(D)ployer I'd like to (A)tell you
That it's been (Em)wonderful these last few (G)years

For the end of the song (the "that's the reason" parts), the chord progression changes slightly to:

D A C G

The timing of the chords in this section goes something like this:
(D)That's the reason (A)that I quit
The (C)reason that I (G)quit
