These fingerings should be close enough to get you rolling.
And, seeing the the _Sauget_Wind_ 7" jacket art pictures Jay's
harp in a holder, I felt obligated to take a cut at the
harmonica tab as well.

I did my best to relate the rhythm (chords written twice should
be played for two full beats) as best I could.  If anyone has
input on how to more accurately do this, please let me know.

For the harmonica part, you'll need a blues harp in the key of
G.  On the harp tabs, the number corresponds to the holes where
numbers run from left to right, 1 to 10.  "x" means exhale, "i"
means inhale.

Let me just say, figuring this song was a blast.  Give it a
try.  Let me know what you think.  I love this stuff.
