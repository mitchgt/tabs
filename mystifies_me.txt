There are two guitars in this song. The rhythm guitar has a 
capo on the 1st fret, and the lead (a la Keith Richards) has
a capo on the 4th fret. The rhythm guitar's chords are:

--2-----1^2---4----4----1------------------------------------

--3-----3-----1----1----3------------------------------------
--4-----1-----1----3----3------------------------------------
-------------------4---(1)-----------------------------------

  Db   Bbm    Eb   Ab   Fm

In terms of open-chord shapes, it's:
Db:  C shape
Bbm: Am shape
Eb:  D shape
Ab:  G shape
Fm:  Em shape

Remember that it's capo 1st fret, so those 1s are just open 
strings. With the exception of the intro, I'll use these chords
as a template to follow the lyrics and lead guitar.
